Mark Zuckerberg


Personal Information

Full name:

Mark Elliot Zuckerberg.

Born:

May 14, 1984, White Plains, New York, United States.


Early Life and Education

Early Coding:

He showed an early aptitude for programming. By age 12, he created "ZuckNet", a messaging program that allowed
his father's dental office and their home computers to communicate.

Education:

He attended Phillips Exter Academy before enrolling at Harvard University in 2002 to study computer science.


The Founding of Facebook

Early Projects:

While at Harvard, he built Course Match and FaceMash, the latter of which allowed users
to compare the photos of students of campus.

The Launch:

On February 4, 2004, he launched Thefacebook.com with his roommates
Eduardo Saverin, Dustin Moskovitz, and Chris Hughes.

Expansion:

The network was originally limited to Harvard students but quickly expanded to
other universities and eventually to the public, growing into a massive social platform.


Rise of Meta and Leadership

Going Public:

Facebook became a publicly traded company on May 18, 2012.

Meta Rebrand:

In October 2021, Zuckerberg renamed the parent company to Meta. This shifted the strategic focus to
building immersive social experiences, virtual and augmented reality, and the metaverse.

Key Acquisitions:

Under his leadership, Meta acquired major digital platforms, including
Instagram(2012), and WhatsApp(2014).


Personal Life and Philanthrophy

Marriage:

He married Priscilla Chan in May 2012, and they have children together.

Chan Zuckerberg Initiative (CZI):

In 2015, Zuckerberg and his wife founded CZI. the philantrophic organization leverages technology
to address global challenges, including reforming the criminal justice system, improving education,
and eradicating disease.
