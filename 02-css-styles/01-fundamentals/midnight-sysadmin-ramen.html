Midnight Sysadmin Ramen

A high-soudium, low-effort upgrade to satndard instant noodles, optimized for late-night compilation sessions, long debugging marathons, or deep-focues study blocks.

Execution time: 5 minutes total runtime.

  • 1 pack of spicy instant ramen noodles.
  • 1 soft-boiled egg (dropped in during the final loop).
  • Freshly chopped scallions and a drizzle of sesame oil for configuration optimization.

Pro Tip: Drop a slice of American cheese directly into the hot broth while it is off the burner. It sounds chaotic, but it melts into aperfectly creamy upgrade patch for the soup.
